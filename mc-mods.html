These are some mods I created for Minecraft. Creating mods has taught me a lot about programming, but also about designing content for games, how to use the hooks and systems that are given to me and how to build upon basic mechanics to improve them.

Buzzy Drones

Buzzy Drones is a mod that adds content to improve the technical part of the game, providing a system to transfer items between containers.

Reworked Metals

Reworked Metals is a mod that rewrites the game's smelting system to give a better sense of progression through the entire game.
