Hi, my name is Nico, and I'm a self-taught game developer with a passion for indie games.

I'm currently studying Computer Science at the university of Bolzano, Italy, and making games in my free time. Learning game design made me notice how much thought goes into making a game, how objects and lights are strategically placed in the environment, how important is the structure of a level, and how many iterations it takes to create a flowing gameplay. In all the games I play I notice details that go unnoticed to most people, I learn from them and I incorporate them in my games, trying to improve myself every time, always learning new things.
